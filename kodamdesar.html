Kodamdesar Bheruji is a renowned temple dedicated to Lord Bheruji (Bhairav) located near Bikaner, Rajasthan, India. Here are some key details about Kodamdesar Bheruji:

Religious Significance-
- Deity: The temple is dedicated to Lord Bheruji, an incarnation of Bhairav, who is considered a fierce manifestation of Lord Shiva. He is revered as a protector and guardian deity.
- Devotion: Bheruji is worshipped by many devotees who seek his blessings for protection, prosperity, and the fulfillment of their wishes.

Location-
- Proximity: The temple is situated approximately 24 kilometers from Bikaner, making it accessible for pilgrims and tourists.
- Setting: The temple is located in a serene rural setting, providing a tranquil atmosphere for worship and reflection.

Architecture and Features-
- Temple Structure: The temple is simple yet significant, reflecting the traditional architectural style of rural Rajasthan.
- Murti (Idol): The idol of Bheruji is adorned with traditional Rajasthani attire and ornaments, and devotees often offer red vermilion (sindoor) and other offerings.

Cultural Practices-
- Offerings: Devotees commonly offer alcohol, meat, and red vermilion to the deity, which is a unique aspect of Bhairav worship. These offerings are believed to appease the deity.
- Festivals: Special festivals and fairs are held at the temple, attracting large crowds of devotees. These events are marked by traditional rituals, music, and dance, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Pilgrimage-
- Devotee Visits: Kodamdesar Bheruji Temple is a popular pilgrimage site for the local population and people from nearby areas. Pilgrims visit the temple to seek blessings and fulfill vows.
- Local Beliefs: There are many local legends and stories associated with the temple and its miraculous powers, enhancing its spiritual significance.

Accessibility-
- Transportation: The temple is well-connected by road, and visitors can easily reach it by private vehicles or local transport from Bikaner.
- Facilities: Basic facilities for pilgrims, such as parking and rest areas, are available near the temple.

Kodamdesar Bheruji Temple stands as an important religious and cultural landmark in the Bikaner region, embodying the deep-rooted spiritual traditions of Rajasthan.
